Card Archive / navbar study

Four navigation directions without the button-chip feel.

These keep the Card Archive page body language, but test navbars that feel lighter and more integrated. Option 1 is the colored underline approach; the other three use rule lines, index tabs, and card-corner markers.

Option 1

Suit Underline

The cleanest approach: plain text links, with the active or hovered route marked by that page's suit color.

Best partElegant and easy to carry across every page.
Corner feelNo nav boxes; page cards still carry the slight rounding.
Use whenYou want the body design to stay prominent.
